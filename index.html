Our Story

Art Born from Patience & Passion

ThreadSphere was born out of a deep love for geometry, colour, and the meditative rhythm of hand-craft. Each piece begins with a mathematical blueprint and comes alive through hundreds of carefully placed nails and thousands of hand-wound threads.

We believe art should feel alive — should pull you in, make you look closer, and reveal new patterns every time. That is the ThreadSphere promise.
